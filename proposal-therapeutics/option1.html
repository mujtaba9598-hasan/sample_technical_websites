About Us

Where Clinical Expertise Meets Compassionate Care

Luminous Therapeutics Medical Massage is built around one idea: therapeutic bodywork should be precise, personalized, and accountable. Every client receives a treatment plan tailored to their unique condition and goals, with progress tracked from session to session.

We work with both chronic conditions — like degenerative disc disease, chronic fatigue syndrome, and migraines — and acute issues such as athletic injuries, car accident recovery, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Our team harnesses the body's own healing ability through clinical-grade techniques that go far beyond surface-level relief.

As a small, focused practice, we give every client the time and attention they deserve. That personal touch, combined with advanced clinical knowledge, is what sets Luminous Therapeutics apart.
