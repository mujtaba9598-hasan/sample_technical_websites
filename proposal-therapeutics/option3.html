About Us

Precise. Personalized. Accountable.

Luminous Therapeutics Medical Massage is built around one idea: therapeutic bodywork should be precise, personalized, and accountable. Every client receives a treatment plan tailored to their unique condition and goals, with progress tracked from session to session.

They work with chronic conditions -- degenerative disc disease, chronic fatigue syndrome, migraines -- and acute issues like athletic injuries, car accident recovery, and post-surgical rehabilitation. As a small, focused practice, every client gets the time and attention they deserve.
