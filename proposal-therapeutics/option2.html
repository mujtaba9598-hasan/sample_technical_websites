About Luminous Therapeutics

Precise, personalized, and accountable.

Luminous Therapeutics Medical Massage is built around one idea: therapeutic bodywork should be precise, personalized, and accountable. Every client receives a treatment plan tailored to their unique condition and goals, with progress tracked from session to session.

We work with chronic conditions — degenerative disc disease, chronic fatigue syndrome, migraines — and acute issues like athletic injuries, car accident recovery, and post-surgical rehabilitation. As a small, focused practice, we give every client the time and attention they deserve.

1

In-Depth Assessment

We evaluate your condition, history, pain patterns, and range of motion before treatment begins.

2

Custom Protocol

Your plan is built around your diagnosis with clear milestones and measurable goals.

3

Track & Adapt

We measure improvement every session and adjust the plan as your body responds.
